Artist Statement

The Hansen’s is an exhibition that gives a brief look into my life, my family, and my driving force that keeps me going.

By now I’m sure you noticed this is my family. My goal in this is to have you the audience see my family threw my eyes, I will achieve this through my use of light, depth of field and patience… lots of patience.

This exhibit will depict the simple pleasures that come with being around people, your family, your friends.

I want you to look at watching TV, doing your hair, playing a game as moments worth hanging on a wall, to me art is meant to be hung on a wall.
