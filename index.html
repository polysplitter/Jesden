The Hansens

The Hansens is an exhibition that gives a brief look into what I believe is my strongest asset, the thing gives me wealth, and pushes me to be better and keep going. By now I’m sure you have noticed this is my family.

My goal in this is to have you the audience see my family through my eyes, I will achieve this through my use of light, depth of field and patience… lots of patience. This exhibit will depict the simple pleasures that come with being around people, your family, and your friends. I want you to look at watching TV, doing your hair, playing a game, and taking a walk as moments worth hanging on a wall because to me art is meant to be hung on a wall.

“If you look at something long enough, you will find something interesting in it.”

-ME
